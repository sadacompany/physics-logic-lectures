The Pendulum

Swing a pendulum from any angle and length. Measure the period and compare it to the small-angle approximation T = 2 pi sqrt(L/g). Explore how large angles cause the period to deviate from theory.

What's Happening

Press Play to release the pendulum from the initial angle. The restoring force is proportional to sin(theta), not theta itself. For small angles these are nearly equal, making the motion approximately simple harmonic.
