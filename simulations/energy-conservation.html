Conservation of Energy

Watch a ball roll along a curved track as kinetic and potential energy continuously transform into each other. Add friction to see energy dissipate as heat, violating conservation only in appearance.

What's Happening

Press Play to release the ball from the set initial height. The ball rolls along a valley-shaped track. Without friction, total mechanical energy is perfectly conserved. Add friction to see energy slowly dissipate.
